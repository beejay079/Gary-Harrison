About the Author

Gary Harrison

Gary Lewis Harrison, born into the turbulent life of a military family, shares his haunting yet hopeful memoir, "HELP." This poignant narrative chronicles the profound struggles and emotional battles faced by a family torn apart by violence and the indifference of the military institution.

From the earliest memories of his mother's love and sacrifice to the harrowing experiences of domestic abuse, Gary's story is one of survival against all odds. The author recounts the pain inflicted by his father, a decorated soldier whose personal demons and untreated trauma from war wreaked havoc on the family. Despite repeated pleas for help, the military's failure to intervene left deep scars that Gary and his siblings carry to this day.

Welcome to the official website of Gary Harrison, author of HELP “Seeking Justice, Seeking Peace.” This is more than just a book site — it's a space for readers, thinkers, and seekers of truth. Here, you’ll discover the heart behind the words: a passion for justice, a voice for the unheard, and a message of peace in turbulent times. Whether you're a fellow writer, a curious reader, or someone drawn to stories with purpose, you'll find something meaningful here. Share the message. Be part of the movement for justice and peace.

"HELP" is not just a tale of suffering; it is also a testament to the resilience of the human spirit. Through reflections on his troubled past, Gary seeks forgiveness and understanding, both for himself and for his father. He navigates the complex emotions of love, anger, and redemption, ultimately finding a path to healing and peace.
